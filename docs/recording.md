# Recording

Record talkgroup audio and play it back later. Enable recording in **Settings > Recording**.

## Starting a Recording

Once enabled, recording controls appear in the top-right corner of the PTT screen while connected to a server.

Tap the **record** button to start. A red timer shows elapsed time in `MM:SS` format. Tap the button again to stop. The recording is saved automatically.

Recordings capture the talkgroup audio as you hear it — both incoming and outgoing transmissions.

## Browsing Recordings

Tap the **folder** icon next to the record button to open the Recordings screen. Each recording shows:

- **Date and time** of the recording
- **Talkgroup** number (with preset label if configured)
- **Duration** in minutes and seconds
- **File size**

Recordings are sorted newest-first.

## Playback

Tap a recording to play it. A progress bar appears below the card showing the current position. Tap again to stop playback. Only one recording can play at a time.

## Sharing

Each recording has a **share** button that opens the standard Android share sheet. Send recordings via email, messaging apps, cloud storage, or any other share target on your device.

Recordings are saved as standard WAV files (16 kHz, 16-bit PCM mono) — compatible with any audio player or editor.

## File Details

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Format** | WAV (uncompressed PCM) |
| **Sample rate** | 16 kHz |
| **Bit depth** | 16-bit |
| **Channels** | Mono |
| **Approx. size** | ~2 MB per minute |
| **Filename** | `TG{number}_{YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS}.wav` |

Recordings are stored in VoxLink's private app directory. They are not visible in the Android Files app — use the in-app Recordings screen to manage them.

> To free up space, swipe a recording to delete it. Deleted recordings cannot be recovered.
